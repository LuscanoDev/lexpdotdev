my projects and tools

this is where i put my projects and tools that i developed. maybe this will look a bit better in the future.

MicroBash
microbash is a mini program made in MicroPython to simulate a bash command line on devices like esp8266 and raspberry pi picos.
CubeClicker
cubeclicker is a game based on cookie clicker, but with squares instead of cookies. your goal is to reach Infinity points.
3 random letters every 5 minutes
a bluesky bot that posts 3 random letters every 5 minutes. sometimes it can send funny things or just weird stuff.
MeiaUm's Progress Bar
a progress bar that shows how much meiaum's subathon has done and still needs to do. made with a group of devs from meiaum's community

maybe some projects here don't work. maybe my power went out or the server crashed... let me know!
